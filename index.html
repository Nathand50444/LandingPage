Explore the world of hounds.

Many types. Many personalities.
The Greyhound was originally bred for coursing, racing and hunting. However, large-scale adoption of retired racing Greyhounds, has allowed the breed to see a resurgence in popularity as a family pet.
The Whippet closely resembles the Greyhound but has the highest running speed of any breed of its weight, and may have the fastest acceleration of any dog.
A Lurcher is a crossbreed typically between a sighthound and a working dog. The lurcher was bred to be a smarter, adept hunting dog for rabbits, hares, and game birds due to the combination of speed and intelligence.
The Saluki was originally bred in the Fertile Crescent of the Middle East (Iraq, Syria, Lebanon, etc). Historically, the ancestors of the modern Saluki breed were used for hunting by nomadic tribes.
